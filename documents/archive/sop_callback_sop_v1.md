# Callback SOP

**Company:** StashSage for Path of Exile 2 (POE2)  
**Business version:** version: 1  
**Effective:** July 21, 2025 through January 6, 2026

Purpose: Provide the standard procedure for handling callbacks for StashSage for Path of Exile 2 (POE2), an HVAC business.

1. **Receive the callback request.** Operations receives the request and treats it as a callback requiring follow-up.

2. **Review the request.** Operations reviews the information available in the callback request before taking action.

3. **Follow up.** Operations contacts the requester using the available callback information.

4. **Record the outcome.** Operations records the result of the callback and any follow-up needed.

5. **Complete the callback.** Operations closes the callback after the outcome has been recorded and any identified follow-up has been addressed.

This SOP is Version 1 and is effective during 2025 from July 21, 2025 through January 6, 2026.
